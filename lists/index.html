Cooking rice

  1. Put rice into rice cooker bowl.
  2. Rinse rice with warm water until milky appearance of water is clear.
  3. Place rice cooker bowl into rice cooker.
  4. Put lid on top.
  5. Press down on rice cooker button.
  6. Once red light turns orange and setting turns to 'Warm' rice is done ~15 mins.
  7. Enjoy!
